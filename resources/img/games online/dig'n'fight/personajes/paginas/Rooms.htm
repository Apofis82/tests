Dungeon Heart

Your heart beats here - in the center of your dungeon. Protect it! Do not permit anyone to approach it. Defend it and annihilate anything and everything that could threaten it!
Requirements: None
Costs: 1 = 1000

--------------------------------------

Entrance

The gaping maw of Hell, leading to all other levels! Creatures from all unholy places can reach your dungeon by means of the entrace, if you offer enough appropriate incentives!
Requirements:
a) 1x1Sleeping quarters
b) 1x1Chicken farm

Costs: 1 = 300

------------------------------------

Treasure chamber

I am pleased to announce that we were able to engage the unique, incomparable and generally well-liked Ruebezahl as Minister of Finance. Please do not be surprised if your inventory display must be corrected occasionally, he is not very good with numbers...
Requirements:
a) 1x1Dungeon heart

Costs: 1 = 200

-------------------------------------

Chicken farm

Warning: chickens on the loose! Do not leave your creatures in this room without supervision! If you do, the consequences maye include: bloodthirstiness, feeding frenzies and devastation!
Requirements:
a) 1x1Dungeon heart

Costs: 1 = 300

-------------------------------------

Sleeping quarters

Your creatures sleep here after they performed their duties for the day. Cutting Z's is all part of a day's work and someone once said that all the sawdust the piles up as a result is sent directly to Hell to stoke the flames of purgatory. I do not believe it... With THAT MUCH sawdust it would be too hot even for the devil himself!
Requirements:
a) 1x1Dungeon heart
b) 1x1Chicken farm

Costs: 1 = 300

----------------------------------

A rolling stone gathers no moss. The early bird catches the worm. Always be prepared and remain one step ahead of your rivals!

a) 4x4Chicken farm
b) 3x3Sleeping quarters

Library

Torture methods from A for arm fractures to Z for zanzhi, spell rolls, magical formulas and recipes for preparing chickens... and chicken courts.
Requirements:
a) 4x4Sleeping quarters
b) 3x3Training room

Costs: 1 = 1000

------------------------------------

Workshop

Your creatures are like little children, they are curious and want to explore the world! From time to time they even manage to develop something useful, such as a new and creative type of torture device for your dungeon.
Requirements:
a) 6x6Sleeping quarters
b) 4x4Training room
c) 4x4Library

Costs: 1 = 1000

-----------------------------------

Guard room

Beware! Your rivals are en route to your dungeon! To protect yourself effectively you need trained and thoroughly battle-tested creatures.
Requirements:
a) 5x5Training room
b) 5x5Workshop

Costs: 1 = 1500

-------------------------------

Arena

When the sound of clattering swords fills the air, when sweat and blood mix in the sand, when two duellists mercilessly and unceasingly battle one another simply because they enjoy the slaughter, an imp swiftly seeks shelter far, far away from the fray.
Requirements:
a) 8x8Chicken farm
b) 7x7Sleeping quarters
c) 6x6Training room
d) 4x4Guard-room

Costs: 1 = 1200

----------------------------------

Casino

Here your creatures can merrily gamble away their worldy goods. Since they rarely possess anything and are poor gamblers, they most often gamble away their very souls. As you can well imagine, the devil is quite pleased about each new casino that appears in the underworld.
Requirements:
a) 9x9Treasure chamber
b) 15x15Chicken farm
c) 7x7Workshop
d) 5x5Arena

Costs: 1 = 3000

----------------------------------

Prison

It smelled like mold, urine and excrement. But even worse was the sight before my eyes: decaying corpses with open guts still moving about in their cells!
Requirements:
a) 11x11Treasure chamber
b) 17x17Chicken farm
c) 6x6Guard-room
d) 6x6Arena

Costs: 1 = 4600

---------------------------------

Torture Chamber

Screams the whole night long, slowly becoming hoarse but still full of power. I found the source of the cries of agony, but I wish I had never have searched for it.... After a short eternity of pure agony I am now sure that nobody has followed my screams (fortunately for them, but sealing my doom).
Requirements:
a) 17x17Treasure Chamber
b) 18x18Chicken Farm
c) 15x15Training Room
d) 7x7Prison

Costs: 1 = 6000

----------------------------------

Graveyard

The groaning sound of opening coffins, gravestones with no inscription, bone dry, ashen ground! Welcome to the sleeping quarters of the undead! Humans first learn to appreciate the peace and tranquility of this place when they are dead.....
Requirements:
a) 20x20Sleeping Quarters
b) 11x11Prison
c) 14x14Workshop
d) 8x8Torture Chamber

Costs: 1 = 7500

Temple

Requirements:
a) 25x25Sleeping Quarters
b) 25x25Chicken Farm
c) 20x20Treasure Chamber
d) 8x8Library
e) 6x6Graveyard

Due to legal regulations I am obliged to inform you of the repercussions of using demonic assistance before you make sacrifices dedicated to HIM. Someone must lose his soul and suffer indescribable agony on his way to Hell! In short: A good thing for the sacrificer, a bad thing for the sacrificee!

Costs: 1 = 10000
